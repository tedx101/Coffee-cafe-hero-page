Our Story

Born from a love of craft

Myme began with a simple belief — that great coffee deserves patience. Nestled in the heart of Laxmi Nagar, Nagpur, we source single-origin beans from farms across India and the world, roasting each batch to draw out its truest character.

Our baristas are devotees of the craft. Every pour-over, every espresso, every cold brew is prepared with intention — because the ritual of making coffee is just as important as drinking it.

“Patience is the secret ingredient. In coffee, as in everything.”

The Space

Step inside and the world softens

Warm wood, the quiet hum of the grinder, soft light falling through wide windows — Myme is designed to be your pause. Whether you're here to work, read, or simply sit with your thoughts, this is your space.

We keep things intentionally unhurried. No rush, no noise, just the gentle rhythm of a space that was made for you.
